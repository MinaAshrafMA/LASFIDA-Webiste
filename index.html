About Us

Samih Mohammed Al-Ghazali
(LaSFIDA'S CEO)
holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Harvard University and is a distinguished leader in his field. He has held numerous executive positions in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, demonstrating extensive expertise in strategic management and business development. His leadership has contributed significantly to the success of the organizations he has worked with. Committed to innovation, he continuously strives to enhance corporate performance by implementing the latest management practices.

Company Activities:-
Our company operates across multiple sectors, specializing in exporting local products to global markets. We take pride in promoting the rich diversity of goods from our region and ensuring their reach to consumers worldwide. In addition to our export efforts, we excel in real estate marketing and finishing services, offering comprehensive solutions that cater to both property developers and buyers.
Our expertise in these fields allows us to build strong connections between local producers and international clients while enhancing the appeal of real estate projects through effective marketing strategies and high-quality finishes. Through our diverse operations, we remain committed to driving growth and success for our clients in both the export and real estate sectors. Our company specializes in exporting a wide range of products within the Arab Republic of Egypt, including: Agricultural Products: Vegetables (Fresh, Frozen, and Dried) Fruits (Fresh, Frozen, and Dried) Legumes and Grains Dairy Products Flour and Sesame Oils
International Trade Brokerage: In addition to exports, we engage in international trade brokerage for essential commodities, including:-
Wheat (Russian & Ukrainian)
Brazilian Sugar
Brass Sheets
